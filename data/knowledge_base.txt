## Compliance Questions

### Q: What is GDPR?
A: The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a European Union regulation on data protection and privacy. It applies to any organization that processes personal data of EU residents, regardless of where the organization is located.

### Q: What is ISO 27701?
A: ISO 27701 is an extension to ISO 27001 that provides guidance for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving a Privacy Information Management System (PIMS).

### Q: What are the CBJ data protection requirements?
A: The Central Bank of Jordan has established data protection and cybersecurity requirements for financial institutions operating in Jordan. These include data governance frameworks, security controls, and privacy protections. We help organizations align with these requirements.

### Q: What is a DPIA?
A: A Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) is a process to help identify and minimize data protection risks of a project or system. It is required under GDPR for processing that is likely to result in high risk to individuals.

## Security Questions

### Q: What is the difference between vulnerability assessment and penetration testing?
A: 
- **Vulnerability Assessment (VA):** Systematic scanning and analysis to identify security weaknesses. Provides a broad view of vulnerabilities.
- **Penetration Testing (PT):** Simulated attacks to exploit vulnerabilities and demonstrate real-world impact. Goes deeper than VA.

We clearly separate these services with no overlap.

### Q: What is IAM?
A: Identity and Access Management (IAM) is a framework of policies and technologies ensuring the right individuals access the right resources at the right times for the right reasons.

### Q: What is PAM?
A: Privileged Access Management (PAM) is a subset of IAM focused on controlling and monitoring access for users with elevated privileges (administrators, IT staff, etc.).
